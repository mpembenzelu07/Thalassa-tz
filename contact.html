Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our snorkeling tours

What should I bring for a snorkeling tour?

We recommend bringing: swimwear, towel, sunscreen (reef-safe), hat, sunglasses, and a change of clothes. We provide all snorkeling equipment including mask, snorkel, fins, and life vest.

Do I need to know how to swim?

Basic swimming ability is recommended but not required for all tours. We offer beginner-friendly tours in shallow, calm waters with life vests provided. Our guides are trained to assist non-swimmers.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refund for cancellations made 48 hours in advance. 50% refund for 24-48 hour notice. No refund for cancellations within 24 hours. We offer rescheduling for weather-related cancellations.

Are children allowed on tours?

Yes! We welcome children ages 6 and up. Children 6-12 must be accompanied by an adult. We offer special family tours with calmer conditions and additional safety measures.

What happens in case of bad weather?

Your safety is our priority. If weather conditions are unsafe, we will reschedule your tour or provide a full refund. We monitor weather conditions closely and make decisions the morning of each tour.
