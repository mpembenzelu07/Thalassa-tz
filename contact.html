BOOKING FAQ

Common questions about booking with Thalassa Dive Center

What happens after I submit the booking form?

We'll contact you within 1-2 hours (during business hours) to confirm availability, provide pricing details, and answer any questions. Once confirmed, we'll send a payment link to secure your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), mobile payments (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa), bank transfers, and cash payments in Tanzanian Shillings or US Dollars.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before your scheduled activity. 50% refund for cancellations 24-48 hours before. No refund for cancellations within 24 hours. Full refund if we cancel due to weather or safety reasons.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes! We offer pickup and drop-off service from most hotels in Dar es Salaam city center for an additional fee. Please mention in your booking if you need transportation.

What should I bring for my dive/snorkel?

We recommend: swimwear, towel, sunscreen (reef-safe), hat, sunglasses, change of clothes, waterproof camera, and any personal medications. We provide all diving/snorkeling equipment.
