POPULAR DIVE SITES

Our most requested dive locations around Dar es Salaam

Bongoyo Island

Marine reserve with vibrant coral gardens and abundant marine life. Perfect for beginners and photographers.

Max Depth: 18m
Duration: 40min
Visibility: 15-25m
Level: Beginner

Common Sightings:

Sea Turtles Parrotfish Clownfish Moray Eels Stingrays
BOOK THIS DIVE

Fungu Yasin Reef

Advanced dive site with dramatic drop-offs and larger marine species. Strong currents - experienced divers only.

Max Depth: 35m
Duration: 35min
Visibility: 20-30m
Level: Advanced

Common Sightings:

Reef Sharks Eagle Rays Barracuda Tuna Grouper
BOOK THIS DIVE

Mbudya Island

Family-friendly site with calm, shallow waters perfect for snorkeling and beginner dives.

Max Depth: 12m
Duration: 50min
Visibility: 10-20m
Level: Beginner

Common Sightings:

Clownfish Butterflyfish Sea Stars Anemones Coral Gardens
BOOK THIS DIVE
