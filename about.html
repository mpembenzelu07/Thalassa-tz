WELCOME TO THALASSA

Your gateway to Tanzania's underwater wonders since 2008

Founded in 2008 by marine biologist and PADI Master Instructor Ali Hassan, Thalassa Dive Center began as a small passion project on the shores of Dar es Salaam. What started as a single boat operation has grown into Tanzania's premier PADI-registered dive center, welcoming thousands of divers and snorkelers from around the world.

Our name, "Thalassa" (θάλασσα), comes from the ancient Greek word for "sea" - a tribute to the timeless connection between humanity and the ocean that has inspired us for millennia.

Located at the beautiful Maga Beach in the Slipway Area of Masaki, we're perfectly positioned to access Dar es Salaam's best dive sites while offering a comfortable base for our guests.

OUR MISSION

To provide safe, unforgettable underwater experiences while promoting marine conservation and sustainable tourism in Tanzania.

OUR VISION

To become East Africa's leading dive center for education, conservation, and exceptional underwater adventures.
