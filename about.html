OUR JOURNEY

From humble beginnings to becoming Tanzania's leading dive center

2008

The Beginning

Founded by marine biologist Ali Hassan with a single boat and a passionate vision to share Tanzania's underwater wonders with the world. What started as a small operation with just two instructors and one dive boat has grown into Dar es Salaam's premier diving destination.

2010

PADI Certification

Achieved official PADI Dive Center status, allowing us to offer internationally recognized certifications and training programs. This milestone opened doors to the global diving community and set new standards for diving excellence in Tanzania.

2013

New Location

Moved to our current state-of-the-art facility at Maga Beach, Slipway Area. This purpose-built center includes modern classrooms, equipment storage, and direct beach access, providing an unparalleled diving experience for our clients.

2016

Expansion

Added new boats, expanded our team, and introduced advanced diving courses and specialized tours. We became the first dive center in Tanzania to offer nitrox diving and underwater photography courses.

2019

5-Star Rating

Achieved PADI 5-Star Dive Center status, the highest rating for dive centers worldwide. This recognition reflects our commitment to excellence in safety, training, and customer service.

2024

Today & Beyond

Serving thousands of divers annually while pioneering marine conservation efforts in Tanzania. We continue to innovate with new technology, expanded services, and commitment to sustainable diving tourism.

OUR CORE VALUES

The principles that guide everything we do at Thalassa Dive Center

Our Mission

To provide safe, unforgettable underwater experiences while promoting marine conservation and sustainable tourism in Tanzania. We create lifelong memories while protecting the ocean for future generations.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading dive center for education, conservation, and exceptional underwater adventures. We aim to set the global standard for responsible diving tourism.

Our Promise

Every dive with us is backed by safety, expertise, and passion. We promise personalized attention, professional service, and an experience that exceeds your expectations.
