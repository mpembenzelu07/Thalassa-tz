OUR JOURNEY

From humble beginnings to becoming Tanzania's leading dive center

The Beginning

Founded by marine biologist Ali Hassan with a single boat and a passion for sharing Tanzania's underwater wonders with the world.

2008

PADI Certification

Became an official PADI Dive Center, offering internationally recognized certifications and training programs.

2010

New Location

Moved to our current location at Maga Beach, Slipway Area, establishing a permanent home for our operations.

2013

Expansion

Added new boats, expanded our team, and introduced advanced diving courses and specialized tours.

2016

5-Star Rating

Achieved PADI 5-Star Dive Center status, the highest rating for dive centers worldwide.

2019

Today & Beyond

Serving thousands of divers annually while pioneering marine conservation efforts in Tanzania.

2023

OUR MISSION

To provide safe, unforgettable underwater experiences while promoting marine conservation and sustainable tourism in Tanzania. We believe in creating lifelong memories while protecting the ocean for future generations.

OUR VISION

To become East Africa's leading dive center for education, conservation, and exceptional underwater adventures. We aim to set the standard for responsible diving tourism in the region.

OUR PROMISE

Every dive with us is backed by safety, expertise, and passion. We promise personalized attention, professional service, and an experience that exceeds your expectations.
