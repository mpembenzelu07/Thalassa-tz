FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Get answers to common questions about snorkeling with us

Do I need to know how to swim to snorkel?

+

Basic swimming ability is required for snorkeling. We provide life vests for all participants, and our guides stay close to assist beginners. For non-swimmers, we recommend trying our beginner tours in shallow, calm waters.

What equipment is provided?

+

We provide all necessary snorkeling equipment: mask, snorkel, fins, and life vest. For tours, we also include waterproof bags, towels, and refreshments. Underwater cameras are available for rent.

What should I bring?

+

Bring swimwear, sunscreen (reef-safe recommended), a hat, and a change of clothes. We recommend bringing a waterproof camera if you have one. All other equipment is provided.

Are there age restrictions?

+

Children must be at least 5 years old to snorkel. Those under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. We offer special family tours suitable for children ages 5-12.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Full refund for cancellations made at least 48 hours before the tour. 50% refund for cancellations made 24-48 hours before. No refund for cancellations within 24 hours or no-shows.
