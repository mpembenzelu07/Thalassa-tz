SNORKELING TIPS

Expert advice for your best snorkeling experience

01

Breathe Calmly

Relax and breathe slowly through your mouth. Don't bite down hard on the snorkel mouthpiece. Practice breathing on the surface before putting your face in the water.

02

Clear Your Mask

If water gets in your mask, look up, press the top of the mask against your forehead, and blow out through your nose. The water will drain from the bottom.

03

Conserve Energy

Use gentle, slow kicks with your fins. Let the fins do the work. Don't thrash or kick rapidly - this scares fish away and tires you out quickly.

04

Look, Don't Touch

Never touch corals or marine life. Corals are living organisms that can be damaged by touch. Some marine creatures can sting or bite if threatened.

05

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water before and after snorkeling. Sun exposure and salt water can dehydrate you quickly. We provide drinking water on all tours.
