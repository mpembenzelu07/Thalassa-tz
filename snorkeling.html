FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before your snorkeling adventure

Do I need to know how to swim to snorkel?

Basic swimming skills are recommended but not mandatory. We provide life vests for all participants and have shallow water options for beginners. Our guides are trained to assist non-swimmers.

What is included in the tour price?

All our snorkeling tours include: full snorkeling gear (mask, snorkel, fins), life vest, professional guide, boat transportation, drinking water, and basic insurance. Some tours also include lunch - check specific tour details.

Is snorkeling safe for children?

Absolutely! We have special child-sized equipment and offer family-friendly tours in calm, shallow waters. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. Our guides are experienced with families.

What should I bring for a snorkeling tour?

We recommend: swimwear, towel, sunscreen (reef-safe), hat, sunglasses, change of clothes, waterproof camera, and any personal medications. We provide waterproof bags for your belongings.
